The Game is Called HOT
QueenKay
Feb 25, 2023 
        Playing With Matches 
        I was a fool for believing you,
        could light me up without burning me down.
        
        Scratch, Scratch, scratch the flame is lit 
        and I dropped my crown.  You frown, I look around-
        to see the upside down smile, turn into a smirk that 
        can go for miles.
            
        The game is called HOT. Try not to drop the flame,
        you let it go and now the spark is remembering my name.
